Acrobat - Self Balancing Robot

Course : MEAM 510 - Design of Mechatronic Systems
Professor : Dr. Jonathan Fiene

Description

The project was aimed at constructing a 2 wheeled robot, which could balance itself based on inputs from an accelerometer and gyroscope. The robot had to be built from the ground up.

Tasks

  • CAD design and fabrication of the robot
  • Design and assembly of electronics for interfacing, sensing and actuation
  • PID control of bot motors based on input from the 2DoF Accelerometer and 1 DoF Gyroscope
